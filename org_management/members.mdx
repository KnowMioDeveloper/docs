## Accessing Members Management

To access the members management page:

1. Click on your current organization name in the sidebar
2. Click on the cog icon next to it
3. Navigate to the **Members** tab

## Members Overview

The Members tab displays all members of your organization. The page shows:

- **Total member count**: The number of members currently in your organization (displayed at the top)
- **Member list**: A list of all organization members with their information

### Member Information

Each member entry displays:

- **Avatar**: A visual identifier (initials or profile picture)
- **Name**: The member's full name
- **Email**: The member's email address associated with the organization

## Managing Members

From this page, you can view all current members and manage their access to your organization.
