## Accessing Teams

To access the teams page:

1. Click on your current organization name in the sidebar
2. Click on the cog icon next to it
3. Navigate to the **Teams** tab

## Teams Overview

The Teams page allows you to organize your organization members into teams for better collaboration and access management.

### Team List

Each team is displayed with the following information:

- **Team name**: The name of the team
- **Created**: The date when the team was created
- **Updated**: The date when the team was last modified

## Managing Teams

From this page, you can view all teams within your organization and manage their members.
